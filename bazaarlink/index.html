Connect & Collaborate with Secure Local Payments

BazaarLink empowers Pakistani creative professionals and small businesses to streamline project collaboration with verified profiles, smart matching, regional language support, and secure payments via bank transfers, Easypaisa, & JazzCash.

Platform Features

  • Local Payment Integration: Supports bank transfers, Easypaisa, and JazzCash for seamless, secure payments in Pakistani rupees between professionals and clients.
  • Language Customization: Allows users to select Urdu or regional languages for messaging, contracts, and UI to accommodate diverse communication needs.
  • Verified Professional Profiles: Professionals create detailed profiles showcasing portfolios, reviews, and local credentials for trust-building.
  • Marketplace Matching Algorithm: Smart matching connects small businesses with relevant creative professionals based on location and service needs.
  • Project Management Dashboard: Provides tools for milestone tracking, messaging, and file sharing tailored to Pakistani business practices.
