Trusted, Secure Rides for Your University Community
CampusRide connects students and staff for convenient on-campus & off-campus transportation with university ID authentication, campus safety integration, and event-based ride coordination.

Platform Features

  • University ID Integration
    Requires university ID authentication for all users, allowing only students and staff to participate to create a trusted, closed network.
  • Campus Safety Integration
    Integrates directly with campus safety services, including a one-tap emergency button that shares ride details and GPS with campus security, plus real-time ride verification for late-night trips (e.g., check-ins every 15 minutes between 10pm-2am).
  • Event-Based Group Rides
    Allows users to create or join rides specifically for campus events, with tailored pickup/drop-off points at event venues, and group ride matching based on event schedules.
  • In-App Messaging with University Policies
    Offers direct messaging limited to confirmed ride parties, auto-moderated with university conduct guidelines, and provides direct escalation to campus authorities if harassment or misconduct is detected.
  • Live Availability Map & Rating System
    Shows real-time map of available rides and drivers within campus boundaries, and includes university-specific ratings for reliability, punctuality, and adherence to campus rules.
