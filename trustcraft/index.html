Barter Skills Securely with TrustCraft

Verified freelancers, smart skill matches, and transparent barter agreements for creative professionals. Say goodbye to unreliable exchanges!

Platform Features
  • Verified Freelancer Profiles
    Identity and portfolio verification for all users, including credentials checks and sample work review to foster trust in barter transactions.
  • Smart Skill Matchmaking
    Algorithm matches freelancers based on complementary skills, availability, and project requirements, reducing time spent manually searching for barter partners.
  • Structured Barter Agreements
    Templated, legally reviewed barter contracts that define scope, deadlines, and deliverables with a built-in mediation process.
  • Barter Deal Progress Tracking
    Milestone-based dashboard for both parties to update progress, submit deliverables, and confirm completion, ensuring accountability throughout.
  • Trust Ratings and Reviews
    Post-project review system that scores partners based on reliability, work quality, and communication, visible on freelancer profiles.
