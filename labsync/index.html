Features

  • Integrated Digital Lab Notebook
    Easily log protocols, results, images, and data files for each experiment. Time-stamped entries keep your scientific records organized and accessible.
  • Custom Lab Assignment Templates
    Leverage pre-built and department-customizable templates for lab reports, collaborative experiments, and data analysis—so your submissions always meet requirements.
  • Collaborative Protocol Tracker
    Assign experiment steps to group members, track progress in real-time, and coordinate responsibilities with comment threads and checklist notifications.
  • Automated Submission & Data Backup Reminders
    Receive smart reminders for file uploads and backups linked to your syllabus dates and project progress—integrated with your university's LMS tools.
  • Version Control for Experiment Data
    Easily view and rollback experiment notes, raw data, and protocols for full scientific traceability and integrity.
