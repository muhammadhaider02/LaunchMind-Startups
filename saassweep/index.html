Platform Features

  • Subscription Overlap Detector
    Uses AI to scan transactions and cross-reference email invoices/account domains, flagging and visualizing SaaS subscriptions with similar features or duplicate billing cycles.
  • One-Click Auto-Cancellation Assistant
    Securely initiates cancellation workflows directly within connected SaaS platforms by auto-filling forms and sending termination emails to providers on your behalf.
  • Project-to-Expense Attribution
    Tags and categorizes each SaaS expense by detecting which project, client, or team initiated the subscription using invoice metadata and user prompts.
  • Personal vs. Reimbursable Subscription Split
    Distinguishes personal and reimbursable SaaS expenses using card identifiers and employer portals, recommending claims or direct cancellations.
  • Unused Tool Notification Engine
    Monitors login frequency and usage via OAuth integrations with SaaS providers, periodically notifying users of subscriptions unused for a user-defined duration with actionable suggestions.
